Zanzibar, an island of spice, history, and leisure

This archipelago blends exotic island culture, historic Stone Town, aromatic spice plantations, and some of the Indian Ocean’s most beautiful beaches.

Signature highlights

  • Stone Town’s UNESCO-listed maze of streets and culture
  • Spice farm tours and local market experiences
  • Dhow sails, snorkeling, and pristine beach resorts

Why Zanzibar captivates travelers

This island combines history, culture, food, and beaches in a laid-back rhythm that feels both exotic and welcoming.

Best known for

Stone Town and spice markets

The labyrinthine alleys, Arab and Swahili architecture, and aromatic spice warehouses transport you to centuries past.

Perfect for

Couples and honeymooners

Zanzibar offers romantic stays, sunset cruises, and unhurried tropical rhythm in a boutique setting.

Add on

Pemba or safari pre-trip

Pair with diving in Pemba or a Tanzanian safari for a fuller East Africa experience.

Suggested flow

  • Day 1: Arrive and settle into a Stone Town guesthouse or waterfront hotel.
  • Day 2: Explore Stone Town’s heritage sites and local markets.
  • Day 3: Spice farm tour and Jozani Forest visit.
  • Days 4–5: Beach resort stays with dhow cruises and snorkeling.
  • Day 6: Departure with island memories.

What makes it special

  • Authentic cultural experiences with local guides
  • World-class island resorts and boutique stays
  • Ideal balance of culture, food, and beach relaxation
