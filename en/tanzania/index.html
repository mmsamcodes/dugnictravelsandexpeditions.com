7 Days in Tanzania

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Morning:

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO).

Transfer to Arusha (about a 1-hour drive) and check in at your hotel.

Relax and acclimatize after your flight.

Afternoon:

Visit the Arusha National Park for a short game drive and a glimpse of wildlife such as giraffes, zebras, and monkeys. The park also offers beautiful views of Mount Meru.

Evening:

Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant in Arusha.

Accommodation: Hotel in Arusha.

Day 2: Arusha to Tarangire National Park

Morning:

After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park (about a 2-hour drive).

Start your game drive in the park, known for its large elephant herds and baobab trees.

Afternoon:

Enjoy a picnic lunch in the park or at a designated area.

Continue your safari in Tarangire, spotting lions, wildebeests, and other wildlife.

Evening:

Check into your lodge or tented camp for the night.

Dinner at the lodge.

Accommodation: Lodge or tented camp in Tarangire.

 

 

Day 3: Tarangire to Ngorongoro Crater

Morning:

After breakfast, head to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area (about 2 hours).

Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a day of game viewing. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is home to a variety of animals, including lions, rhinos, and buffaloes.

Afternoon:

Enjoy a packed lunch in the crater before continuing your game drive.

Evening:

Return to your lodge at the edge of the crater.

Dinner at the lodge.

Accommodation: Lodge near Ngorongoro Crater.

Day 4: Ngorongoro to Serengeti National Park

Morning:

After breakfast, drive towards Serengeti National Park (about a 4-hour drive), stopping for game viewing along the way.

Afternoon:

Arrive in Serengeti and start a game drive as you explore the endless plains of the park.

Serengeti is known for the Great Migration (in season) and a high concentration of predators like lions, cheetahs, and leopards.

Evening:

Check into your lodge or tented camp.

Enjoy a sunset game drive if time allows.

Accommodation: Luxury tented camp or lodge in Serengeti.

Day 5: Full Day in Serengeti

Morning:

Early morning game drive in Serengeti for the best chances to see wildlife, including the "Big Five."

Afternoon:

Return to the lodge for lunch and a short rest.

Afternoon game drive in different parts of the Serengeti.

Evening:

Return to your lodge and enjoy a traditional Tanzanian dinner.

Accommodation: Same as Day 4.
