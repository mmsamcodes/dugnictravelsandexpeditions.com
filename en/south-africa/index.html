JOHANNESBURG

7 Days In South Africa

South Africa offers a mix of natural wonders, cultural richness, and adventure, It blends unforgettable landscapes, thrilling activities, and a deep historical perspective.

Nature: See the "Big Five" on safari at Kruger National Park, enjoy coastal wildlife like penguins at Boulders Beach, and explore Table Mountain.

Cities: Cape Town boasts scenic beauty and wine country, while Johannesburg offers history at the Apartheid Museum and Soweto tours.

Adventure: Dive with sharks, bungee jump at Bloukrans Bridge, or hike the Drakensberg Mountains.

Culture and Food: Experience diverse traditions, Zulu villages, and enjoy local dishes like biltong and world-class wines.

Day 1: Arrival and Welcome

Morning: Arrival at OR Tambo International Airport. Private transfer to a mid range or luxury hotel.

Afternoon: Check-in and relaxation at the hotel.

Evening: Welcome dinner at the hotel restaurant.

Day 2: Johannesburg History and Culture

Morning: Visit the Apartheid Museum and Constitution Hill.

Afternoon: Guided Soweto tour (Nelson Mandela House, Hector Pieterson Memorial). Lunch in Soweto.

Evening: Return to the hotel for dinner.

Day 3: Safari at Pilanesberg Game Reserve

Morning: Early departure for a day safari at Pilanesberg (2.5-hour drive). Morning game drive.

Afternoon: Lunch in the reserve and another game drive.

Evening: Return to Johannesburg. Dinner at the hotel.

Day 4: Lesedi Cultural Village

Morning: Drive to Lesedi Cultural Village. Experience traditional dances, storytelling, and guided village tours.

Afternoon: Lunch included at the village.

Evening: Return to the hotel for relaxation and dinner.

Day 5: Pretoria and Cradle of Humankind

Morning: Drive to Pretoria to visit the Union Buildings and Voortrekker Monument.

Afternoon: Tour of the Cradle of Humankind (Sterkfontein Caves and Maropeng Visitor Centre). Lunch en route.

Evening: Return to the hotel for dinner.

Day 6: Shopping and Relaxation

Morning: Shopping at Sandton City Mall.

Afternoon: Explore the Maboneng Precinct and local art galleries. Lunch in Maboneng.

Evening: Return to the hotel for a farewell dinner.

Day 7: Departure

Morning: Relaxation or optional souvenir shopping near the hotel.

Afternoon: Airport transfer for departure.
