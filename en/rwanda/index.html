4 Days Rwanda

Rwanda, known as the "Land of a Thousand Hills," is a stunning East African country renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and remarkable wildlife. Despite its tragic history, particularly the 1994 genocide, Rwanda has made incredible strides in recovery and is now considered one of Africa's most progressive and peaceful nations.
